My Story

I’m Polina, a UX designer based in Berlin since 2015, originally from St. Petersburg, Russia. My journey into UX design began in October 2023, transitioning from a successful career in accounting and finance, where I worked with a range of companies—from startups to established firms in industries such as logistics, fashion, and tech. I’m driven by the goal of creating digital products that are not only business-efficient but also truly valuable for users.

Free Time: I enjoy reading, hiking, and traveling. I switch between reading fiction and non-fiction, alternating between different languages like Russian, English, and German.
